✂️ Pocket Zine Folding Guide:
  1. Print this sheet in landscape format (landscape option enabled).
  2. Fold the paper in half horizontally, then unfold.
  3. Fold the paper in half vertically, then fold the outer edges to the center crease (8 segments), then unfold.
  4. Cut a slit along the horizontal center line between the two middle segments.
  5. Push the two ends together to pop the slit open, forming a 3D cross, and fold it into an 8-page pocket book!
