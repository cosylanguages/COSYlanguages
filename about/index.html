The COSYlanguages Project

A cosy, immersive language learning platform built with love and open-source principles.

Our Mission

COSYlanguages was born from a simple idea: language learning should be human, speaking-first, and accessible. We are building a platform that focuses on genuine learning over monetisation, providing tools for both independent learners and teachers.
