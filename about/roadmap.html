Project Roadmap 🗺️

Phased development stages shaping physical office spaces, offline tooling, and pedagogical features.

🎨 Phase I: Visual Harmony & Accessibility

Focusing on high-contrast accessibility (WCAG AA/AAA compliance), clean layout, modern typography, responsive visual flows, and seamless student guidance.

Stage Initiative / Feature Details & Impact
✅ Completed Contrast & Accessibility Uplift 💡 Darkened stats card backgrounds (`rgba(26,43,29,0.45)`) with glassmorphic backdrop blur; updated category subtitles and action links to high-contrast emerald greens (`var(--sage-dark)`) for AA/AAA compliance.
✅ Completed Clean Layout & Typography ✍️ Harmonized body paragraph spacing and swapped complex colored paragraph fonts on the landing page for high-legibility dark charcoal colors (`var(--ink-soft)`) over warm sand and gold backgrounds.
✅ Completed Interactive Accessibility Cues 🔗 Polished footer notes and calculator footnotes to use distinct underlined green brand links, featuring micro-interactive smooth color transitions on hover.
✅ Completed Interactive Tour & Compass FAB 🧭 Injected the interactive 9-step floating `🧭 Guide` walkthrough widget to smoothly navigate visitors through core hubs (Games, Practice, Events).

⚡ Phase II: Hybrid Digital & Physical Toolkit

Bridging offline-first desktop apps, dynamic print utilities, multi-user local profile storage, and screen-free study resources.

Stage Initiative / Feature Details & Impact
✅ Completed Student progress tracking 📈 Track completed lessons, scores, streaks per language and level via Local Personal Notebook.
⚡ Active Vocabulary Notebook & Exporter 📓 A fully integrated user dictionary allowing custom word harvested entries, local data exports, and offline practice revision statistics.
⚡ Active PWA Caching & Offline Support 📴 Service worker cache assets management (`sw.js`) to cache lessons, stylesheets, and practice tools to let users learn completely without network.
⚡ Active Printable Leitner Flashcards 🖨️ Leitner Box printable standard templates and print studios to let users print physical cards from their active dictionary lists.
⏳ Planned Pocket-Sized Folding Zines 📖 A custom template to turn any 10-unit vocabulary grid into a printable single-sheet A4 PDF that folds into an 8-page pocket-sized study zine.
⏳ Planned Local Family Profiles 👥 LocalStorage multi-user index storage allowing family members sharing the same computer or tablet to keep independent dictionaries and score streaks.
⏳ Planned Adaptive Dark Mode Theme 🌙 Saveable comfortable native dark theme background option (preserving cozy warm sage accents) for comfortable nocturnal studying.

🔥 Phase III: Analog Spaces & Board Games (High Priority)

The ultimate vision: transitioning COSYlanguages into brick-and-mortar office hubs, tabletop board games, and printed language curricula.

Stage Initiative / Feature Details & Impact
⚡ Active Brick-and-Mortar COSY Office 🏡 Opening physical, quiet, welcoming community workspaces. Browse the Monolingual library books available at our study workspace on our interactive Monolingual Library Browser 📖!
⚡ Active COSYlanguages Book Series 📚 Publishing premium comprehensive handbooks for all active languages. Preview the workbook volumes on our newly published Workbooks Curriculum Catalog 📚!
⚡ Active Physical Tabletop Board Game 🏆 A complete social physical tabletop game designed to drill speaking structures. Print the custom Print-and-Play Board Game Set 🎲 with dynamically loaded card decks!
