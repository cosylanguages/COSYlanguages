Privacy & Data Sovereignty

The quietest, most transparent learning environment online.

🌱 Our Pledge

"We do not collect any data of students nor teachers."

Absolute Data Sovereignty

At COSYlanguages, we believe that education should be free from commercial data collection, tracking, and telemetry. We do not maintain user databases, require login accounts, or log your personal learning progress on cloud servers.

Instead, your entire learning experience — including saved vocabulary lists, grammar practice progress, points, and daily streaks — stays exactly where it belongs: with you, in your browser’s local storage. This ensures you are the absolute owner of your learning records, immune to cloud security breaches or tracking.

No Tracker Cookies

COSYlanguages is cookie-free. We do not use third-party analytics scripts, marketing trackers, or social media pixels that trace your activity across the internet. When you learn here, you do so in total peace.

How to Manage Your Data Locally

Because your data is stored client-side inside your browser, clearing your browser's site cookies/local storage will reset your statistics. To prevent this, always use our Export Data 📥 feature located on your Personal Notebook page to download a lightweight backup file (`.json`). You can import this file anytime to restore your progress on any browser or device.
