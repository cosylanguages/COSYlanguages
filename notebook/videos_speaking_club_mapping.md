Integrating these curated media assets directly enriches conversation prompts, supports pre-session preparation, and drives higher active retention of session vocabulary (the **10-10-10 rule**).

## 2. Detailed Session Integration Strategies & Pedagogical Justifications

### V1 & V12: Propaganda & Discrimination Experiments
* **Source Videos:**
  - V1: Nazi Race Propaganda (Would you survive?) — [Link](https://youtu.be/zm1yDO_nMA4?is=gZkJXsh9yKEnJJGf)
  - V12: Jane Elliott “Blue Eyes - Brown Eyes” Experiment — [Link](https://youtu.be/dLAi78hluFc?is=YQPu99PDf5zeLdXC)
* **Target English Sessions:**
  - `events/sessions/dangerous-blindness-perspective.html` (Dangerous Blindness — The Limit of Perspective)
  - `events/sessions/maze-of-biases.html` (The Maze of Biases)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - Add these video links to the "Resources" or metadata section of both sessions to provide high-impact psychological background material.
  - **Dangerous Blindness:** Integrate **Jane Elliott's experiment (V12)** as a prime case study of "blindness" and "empathy" to answer: *“What is the most dangerous form of social blindness currently affecting our modern society?”*
  - **Maze of Biases:** Use **Nazi Race Propaganda (V1)** to explore extreme forms of cognitive confirmation/social groupthink biases under *Round 1, Question 10* (*"Which bias do you think is the most dangerous in the modern world?"*).

### V2: Propaganda and Children (Russian)
* **Source Video:** Что наука знает о влиянии пропаганды на детей и подростков — [Link](https://youtu.be/YMrSF8_OSGk?is=9-HrTRr3cV-lJcZd)
* **Target Sub-Club:** Russian Speaking Club (`events/ru/sessions/`)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - Create a new Russian speaking session titled `vliyanie-propagandy.html` (The Science of Propaganda's Influence on Youth) or map to a localized version of `upbringing-old-vs-modern.html` (Воспитание: прошлое против современности).
  - Use the scientific studies referenced in this video to design Round 2 conditional debate scenarios regarding child media access and critical thinking curricula in schools.

### V3: Non-Verbal Language and Body Expression (Italian)
* **Source Video:** Mi Sono Fatto Prendere la Mano - Italian Gestures — [Link](https://youtu.be/oZk5rAVuNsc?is=EGFHh8egoDzWPuWJ)
* **Target English Sessions:**
  - `events/sessions/losing-spoken-words.html` (Losing Spoken Words)
  - `events/sessions/psychology-of-smiles.html` (The Psychology of Smiles)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - **Losing Spoken Words:** Map this as an interactive pre-session resource. This video perfectly shows how humans communicate complex emotions and abstract concepts *without* spoken words. Use as a prompt for Round 2: *“Future generations will communicate almost entirely through images and short gestures.”*
  - **Italian Sub-Club Expansion:** If an Italian sub-club is introduced, this video serves as the default cultural onboarding session for Beginner (A1) to Intermediate (B1) speakers practicing standard daily expressions.

### V4: Career Satisfaction & Alignment
* **Source Video:** How to Love Your Work — [Link](https://youtu.be/9sVrUTRjjVc?is=lHdOC8q4Z2E-JK1z)
* **Target English Sessions:**
  - `events/sessions/4-day-work-week.html` (The 4-Day Work Week)
  - `events/sessions/national-workaholics-day.html` (National Workaholics Day)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - Enriches the debate on career identity vs personal life. In **The 4-Day Work Week**, this video directly answers the core question: *Does a shorter work week automatically increase career fulfillment, or is loving our work more about changing our emotional relationship with employment?*

### V5 & V9: Defending Language Mistakes (English & Russian)
* **Source Videos:**
  - V5: Why Do Experts Always Defend Language Mistakes — [Link](https://youtu.be/I6duEGj04Mg?is=Wa5eI4c6fsZ_4U2Y)
  - V9: Не исправляй ихнюю речь (Russian) — [Link](https://youtu.be/9ByFlPABPko?is=UK3CvHeo6_vZk3JP)
* **Target Sessions:**
  - English: `events/sessions/pedagogy-and-practice.html` (Pedagogy and Practice) or `events/sessions/losing-spoken-words.html`
  - Russian: `events/ru/sessions/pedagogika-i-praktika.html` (Localized equivalent)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - This is a beautiful cross-linguistic matching! Both videos cover the identical linguistic struggle: **Descriptivism** (accepting natural evolution/slang) vs. **Prescriptivism** (enforcing static grammar guidelines).
  - Integrate these videos in pre-session prompts to trigger debate on whether teachers/experts should correct common errors (like *"their"* vs *"their's"* in English, or *"ихний"* in Russian).

### V10: Evolutionary Role of Grandparents
* **Source Video:** Why Your Grandmother Is an Evolutionary Mystery — [Link](https://youtu.be/iKLwzmjfcW4?is=1l2lHNLpRdMBvJjZ)
* **Target English Session:** `events/sessions/grandparents-mental-health.html` (Why Grandparents Matter)
* **Integration Strategy:**
  - Adds a fascinating scientific/evolutionary dimension to a session that otherwise focuses purely on child psychology. Integrating the "Grandmother Hypothesis" teaches students words like *longevity, natural selection, post-reproductive lifespan,* and *ancestral survival*.
