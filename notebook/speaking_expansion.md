# Speaking Club Expansion Brainstorming

This document outlines how existing speaking club topics can be expanded across different CEFR levels (A1 to C2).

## 1. Keeping Up with Science
*   **Core Topic: Artificial Intelligence**
    *   **A2 (Elementary):** Basic gadgets and voice assistants. "Can robots cook?"
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** AI in the workplace. Will it help or replace us?
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** Deepfakes and misinformation. How to trust what we see.
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** AI and Human Consciousness. Can a machine ever "feel"?

## 2. Let's Celebrate
*   **Core Topic: New Year Traditions**
    *   **A1 (Starter):** Colors, food, and saying "Happy New Year".
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** New Year's resolutions and personal growth.
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** Comparative traditions: Solar vs. Lunar New Year.
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** The sociology of rituals and why humans need collective celebrations.

## 3. The Greatest Quotes
*   **Core Topic: "The unexamined life is not worth living" (Socrates)**
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** Simple self-reflection: "What makes you happy?"
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** The importance of critical thinking in modern media.
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** Existentialism and the search for objective truth in a subjective world.

## 4. Mind Matters
*   **Core Topic: The Power of Habits**
    *   **A2 (Elementary):** Describing daily routines and healthy vs. unhealthy food.
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** Practical tips for building new habits (The 21-day myth).
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** The Habit Loop: Cue, Craving, Response, Reward.
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** Behavioral economics: How companies use our habits to influence decisions.

## 5. My Life With & Without
*   **Core Topic: Social Media**
    *   **A2 (Elementary):** "A day without my phone." Basic apps and communication.
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** Maintaining friendships online vs. offline.
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** The impact of social media on mental health and body image.
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** The Attention Economy: Is privacy dead in the 21st century?

## 6. Debatable & Relatable
*   **Core Topic: The 4-Day Work Week**
    *   **B1 (Intermediate):** Work-life balance and more time for hobbies.
    *   **B2 (Upper-Intermediate):** Productivity metrics: Does working less mean producing more?
    *   **C1 (Advanced):** Universal Basic Income and the decoupling of labor from survival.
