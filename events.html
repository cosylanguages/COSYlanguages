Our Events 🎉

How it works? 💡

Welcome to our Events corner! Here you can find our scheduled clubs and interactive games to practice your language skills.

  • 🎭 Games: Click "Show Games" to see available interactive tools. Perfect for solo or group practice!
  • 🗣️ Speaking Clubs: Check the topics we discuss in our live sessions.
  • 🎤 Immersive Nights: Join us for karaoke or cinema to learn in a fun, cultural way.
  • 👥 Play Together: To play with friends, click "Share" and send the link. The 'Game Code' (or seed in the URL) ensures everyone has the same board or sequence, whether playing online or sitting together offline!
  • 👤 Solo Mode: Most games have a "Solo Mode" or can be played alone to practice at your own pace.

Karaoke Nights 🎤

Sing your heart out in different languages! A fun and interactive way to learn new vocabulary and practice pronunciation.

Cinema Evenings 🎬

Watch movies from around the world in their original language with subtitles. A great way to immerse yourself in the culture and improve your listening skills.

Game Nights 🎲

Play board games, card games, and video games in different languages. A fun and engaging way to learn new words and practice your communication skills.
