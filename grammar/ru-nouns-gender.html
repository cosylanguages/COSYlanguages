Russian Noun Genders
How to identify gender by the ending
Masculine Consonant / -й парк (park), чай (tea)
Feminine -а / -я мама (mama), неделя (week)
Neuter -о / -е окно (window), море (sea)
💡 If a noun ends in a consonant, it is almost always Masculine. If it ends in -а, it is Feminine!
