COSYlanguages Tabletop Board Game 🎲

A custom, print-and-play conversational board game to master language fluency offline.

🎲 How to play:
  1. Print this page (Board Game + challenge cards deck) on thick cardstock or heavy paper. Cut out the 12 playing cards below.
  2. Each player needs a token (e.g. a coin or button). Place them on the 🏠 START space.
  3. Take turns rolling a die or flipping a coin to move forward 1-3 spaces on the winding track.
  4. When you land on a track space, draw a card from the deck and perform the action based on the space:
    • 🎭 Action Hero: Act out or draw the card's word without speaking, other players must guess it!
    • 🗣️ Fluency Flow: Speak continuously for 30 seconds incorporating the card's word!
    • ⚖️ Battle of Wits: Describe the antonym/opposite of the card's word and form a sentence!
    • 🧠 Cognitive Accelerator: Wait exactly 3 seconds, then define/translate the card's word fluently!
  5. First player to reach the 🏁 FINISH space with a correct final challenge wins the game!
