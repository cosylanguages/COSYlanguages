Cardboard Leitner Box Blueprint 📦

Assemble your physical Leitner System spaced-repetition box at home.

✂️ Assembly & Spaced Repetition Instructions:
  1. Print this sheet on heavy A4 paper, cardstock, or glue the printed paper to a thin cereal/cardboard box.
  2. Cut along the solid thick black lines (including the horizontal card compartment slots).
  3. Score and fold along all dashed lines.
  4. Fold up the sides and glue the outer flaps to form an elongated study organizer.
  5. Insert the 5 labeled dividers into the numbered compartment slots to form 5 compartments (Boxes 1-5).
  6. How to play: All new Leitner flashcards start in Box 1. Study them daily: correct answers move up a box (e.g. 1 → 2), incorrect answers fall back to Box 1 immediately. Box 1 studied daily, Box 2 every other day, Box 3 weekly, Box 4 bi-weekly, Box 5 monthly. Reach master fluency!
