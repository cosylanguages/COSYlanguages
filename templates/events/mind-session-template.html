✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
Developer Correction & Feedback Guidelines:
1. Do not cross out or strike through the whole sentence or phrase. Instead, accentuate/highlight/underline the specific error (using <u> tags or background classes) so students see exactly where the grammatical or lexical break occurs.
2. Level-Appropriate Diversification:
- Starter/Elementary (A1-A2): Direct grammatical corrections, spelling, and basic structural alignments (e.g., subject-verb agreement).
- Intermediate (B1-B2): Word choice improvements, better alternatives, and common prepositional collocations.
- Advanced (C1-C2): Sophisticated synonyms, more precise or specific lexical options, and stylistic/register adjustments.
I was sympathizing with her struggles → I was empathizing with her struggles / showed deep empathy (Linguistic register: 'sympathize' suggests feeling pity, while 'empathize' implies a deeper psychological understanding and shared feelings)
We discussed about the psychological impact → We discussed the psychological impact / had a discussion about the psychological impact (Underlined prepositional error. Better collocation: 'discuss' is transitive and takes no preposition, or use 'have a discussion about')
This habit is deeply ingrowed in our brain → This habit is deeply ingrained / embedded in our subconscious (Collocation: 'ingrained' or 'embedded' are the correct advanced adjectives for psychological/behavioural patterns)
