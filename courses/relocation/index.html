Relocation Course

From renting a flat to understanding local bureaucracy and building a new social circle.

Chat on WhatsApp
Duration 90 min
Levels A2 → C1 · Life abroad
Languages EN, FR, IT, RU, EL
Format 1-to-1 Integration First

What you'll cover

  • Navigating the housing market and rental agreements
  • Understanding local administrative and banking systems
  • Essential social skills for making friends and networking
  • Daily life tasks: doctors, utilities, schools, and grocery shopping
  • Cultural integration and understanding local social norms

Who it's for

Expats and anyone planning to move to a new country. If you are feeling overwhelmed by the practical and social challenges of starting a new life in a different language, this course will provide the support you need.

Sample lesson

A Relocation session is intensely practical. One day you might be analyzing a sample rental contract to understand your rights and responsibilities, and the next you'll be role-playing a visit to the local town hall or a conversation at a neighborhood meet-up.

FAQ

Do you help with specific legal advice?
We provide the linguistic tools to understand legal documents, but we are not legal experts. We help you communicate with the professionals who are.
Can we focus on my local city?
Yes, our teachers research local specifics (neighborhoods, slang, local apps) to make your transition as smooth as possible.
How long does the course last?
The course is flexible. Most students take it for 3-6 months surrounding their move, but you can continue as long as you need support.
