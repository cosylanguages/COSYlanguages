Travelling Course

Learn to navigate airports, order in restaurants, and have meaningful chats with locals.

Chat on WhatsApp
Duration 45 min
Levels A1 → B1 · Survival
Languages EN, FR, IT, EL
Format 1-to-1 Journey Focused

What you'll cover

  • Essential phrases for airports, hotels, and transport
  • Ordering food and navigating dietary requirements
  • Asking for directions and understanding the answers
  • Basic cultural etiquette and polite social interactions
  • Emergency vocabulary and how to ask for help

Who it's for

Perfect for holidaymakers and explorers who want to go beyond "just pointing." If you have a trip coming up and want to feel more connected to the local culture, this course will give you the tools to do so.

Sample lesson

A typical lesson focuses on a specific "stop" on your journey. For example, in a "Restaurant" session, you'll role-play ordering a multi-course meal, asking about local specialties, and handling the bill, all while learning the specific vocabulary and cultural nuances of your destination.

FAQ

Can I focus on a specific country?
Yes, we tailor the materials and cultural tips to the specific region you are planning to visit.
Is it okay if I'm a complete beginner?
Absolutely! This course is designed to get you from zero to "functional" very quickly.
Do you cover regional dialects?
We focus on the standard language but will include common regional variations if they are relevant to your destination.
