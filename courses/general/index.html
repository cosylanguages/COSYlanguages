What you'll cover

  • Foundational grammar and syntax through active usage
  • Vocabulary for real-life adult scenarios and interests
  • Listening comprehension across various accents and speeds
  • Reading authentic materials (articles, stories, news)
  • Structured writing practice for personal and semi-formal use
  • Daily speaking practice to build lasting confidence

Who it's for

This course is designed for adult learners who want a solid, comprehensive foundation in their target language. Whether you are starting from zero or looking to reach a near-native level, the General Course provides the structure you need to progress consistently.

Sample lesson

A typical 60-minute session begins with a casual warm-up chat, followed by the introduction of a new linguistic concept through an authentic text or audio. You'll spend the second half of the lesson putting that concept into practice through guided conversation, ensuring that at least 40% of the time you are the one speaking.

FAQ

How long does it take to move up one level?
On average, with two lessons per week and some independent practice, students progress through a sub-level (e.g., A1.1 to A1.2) in about 3-4 months.
Can I switch between 60 and 90-minute lessons?
Yes, you can adjust the duration of your sessions based on your availability and learning goals at any time.
Do I need to buy any textbooks?
No, we provide all necessary digital materials, including custom-made worksheets and access to our online practice tools.
