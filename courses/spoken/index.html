Who it's for

Ideal for busy learners who already have a basic understanding of the language but struggle to speak fluently. If you have "passive knowledge" but feel blocked when it's time to open your mouth, this course is designed for you.

Sample lesson

A Spoken session is intense and focused. There is no "lesson plan" in the traditional sense; instead, your teacher introduces a provocative topic, a piece of news, or a specific scenario, and you spend the entire 15 or 30 minutes in active dialogue, receiving corrections on the fly.

FAQ

Is 15 minutes really enough?
Yes! Frequency is more important than duration for fluency. A 15-minute daily "immersion" session keeps your brain in the target language far more effectively than one long weekly lesson.
Do I need to prepare anything?
No preparation is required. The goal is to simulate real-world spontaneous conversation.
Can I record the sessions?
Absolutely. Many students find it helpful to listen back to their sessions to notice their own progress and review the teacher's corrections.
