Who it's for

Sample lesson

An Exam Prep lesson is highly structured. You might start by analyzing a "Model Answer" for a writing task, followed by an intensive 20-minute mock speaking session where the teacher strictly follows the exam's timing and protocols, ending with a detailed breakdown of your performance against the official rubrics.

FAQ

Do you provide the exam itself?
No, we prepare you for the exam. You will need to register with an official testing center (like the British Council or Alliance Française) to take the test.
Can you guarantee I will pass?
While we cannot guarantee results, our students have a very high success rate because we focus on the specific criteria that examiners are looking for.
How many lessons will I need?
This depends on your current level and your target score. Usually, we recommend starting at least 2-3 months before your exam date.
