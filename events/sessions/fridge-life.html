📖 Session Vocabulary (10 Units)

Preserve
to keep food fresh for a longer time
Example: A fridge helps preserve meat and vegetables.
Spoil
to go bad and become unsafe to eat
Example: Milk spoils quickly without cooling.
Storage
keeping things in a safe place
Example: The fridge is used for food storage.
Freshness
the quality of being fresh and not old
Example: The fridge maintains the freshness of food.
Waste
to use or throw away something unnecessarily
Example: Without a fridge, people waste more food.
Leftovers
food that remains after a meal
Example: I store leftovers in the fridge.
Temperature
how hot or cold something is
Example: The fridge keeps a low temperature.
Convenience
something that makes life easier
Example: A fridge is a great convenience in daily life.
Shop daily
to buy food every day
Example: Without a fridge, people must shop daily.
Hygiene
cleanliness and health safety
Example: A fridge improves food hygiene.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
The Refrigerator Check
  • What is the oldest thing currently in your fridge?
  • What is the one item that is always in your fridge, no matter what?
🔵 Round 1 — My Life WITH a Fridge (10 Questions)▲
Personal Depth
1. How does a fridge help you preserve food and organize your daily life?
★ What foods do you always try to preserve in your fridge?
2. Why is a fridge essential for storing perishable food today?
★ Which perishable food would you miss the most if you couldn't keep it cold?
3. How does proper food storage save time, money and effort?
★ Do you think your fridge helps you save money?
4. How does a fridge help maintain the freshness of food throughout the week?
★ Which foods taste much better when they stay fresh?
5. In what ways does refrigeration reduce food waste?
★ Have you ever managed to avoid waste thanks to your fridge?
6. Why are leftovers useful in a busy adult lifestyle?
★ How often do you eat leftovers instead of cooking again?
7. How has the convenience of refrigeration changed modern family life?
★ What everyday convenience would disappear without your fridge?
8. Why do many people stock up on groceries instead of shopping every day?
★ How often do you stock up on food?
9. Which methods of food preservation would be much less practical than refrigeration today?
★ Have you ever tried preserving food yourself?
10. Do you think the fridge is one of the most important inventions in the modern home? Why?
★ Could you imagine your current lifestyle without owning one?

🟢 Round 2 — My Life WITHOUT a Fridge (10 Units)▲
Speculative Analysis
1. If refrigerators had never existed, what methods would people use to preserve food, and which do you think would become the most popular?
★ Which method of preservation would you trust the most?
2. Without refrigeration, many perishable foods would disappear from supermarkets. Which new dishes or eating habits do you think would replace them?
★ Which food would you miss so much that you'd try to make your own version?
3. If food storage were much more difficult, how would your job, schedule or daily routine change?
★ Would you rather shop every morning or cook everything on the same day?
4. How would people keep food freshness during long journeys, holidays or business trips?
★ Could you enjoy travelling if you had to think about food every few hours?
5. Without refrigerators, would restaurants become more expensive because they couldn't keep leftovers or large amounts of ingredients?
★ Would you eat out more often or cook at home more?
6. If people couldn't stock up on groceries, how would cities, neighbourhoods and local businesses look different today?
★ Would you enjoy living next to a daily food market instead of a huge supermarket?
7. Without refrigeration, would people produce more or less food waste, or would they simply become much better at planning?
★ Do you think you would become more organized if wasting food wasn't an option?
8. Without the convenience of a fridge, what modern invention do you think would have developed much faster to solve the problem?
★ If you were an inventor, what would you create instead of a refrigerator?
9. Imagine that refrigerators had never been invented because electricity was too expensive. How different would modern society be?
★ Do you think people would live slower, healthier or more stressful lives?
10. If the entire world suddenly lost every refrigerator tomorrow, how long do you think society would need to adapt, and what would surprise people the most?
★ What would be the very first thing you would do during the first 24 hours?
