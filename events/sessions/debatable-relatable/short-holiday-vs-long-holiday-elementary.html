Short Holiday vs Long Holiday

18 July 2026

Home / Events / Debate Club / Short Holiday vs Long Holiday
← Back to Club

Duration

60 minutes

Languages

🇬🇧 English

Level

Elementary (A2)

Theme

Travel, Holidays, and Rest

In this session, we discuss the best way to spend our vacation time. Some people prefer taking many short holidays throughout the year, while others love having one long, relaxing trip. We will talk about travel plans, packing bags, and our favorite destinations.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Holiday
a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done.
I love to go to the beach during my summer holiday.
Travel
make a journey, typically of some length.
Many people like to travel to different countries by plane.
Short ≠ Long
measuring a small distance or duration ≠ measuring a great distance or duration.
A short weekend trip is very fun, but a long summer trip is more relaxing.
Vacation
an extended period of recreation, especially one spent away from home or in traveling.
We planned a winter vacation to go skiing in the mountains.
Relax
make or become less tense or anxious.
A warm bath helps me relax after a long day at school.
Destination
the place to which someone or something is going or being sent.
Paris is a very popular tourist destination for people from all over the world.
Plan
a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something.
Do you have a clear plan for your next weekend trip?
Pack
fill a suitcase or bag with clothes and other items needed for travel.
I need to pack my swimsuit and sunglasses for the beach trip.
Active
engaging or ready to engage in physically energetic pursuits.
I prefer an active holiday with hiking and swimming rather than sitting in a hotel.
Choose
pick out or select someone or something as being the best in a situation.
It is always difficult to choose between a mountain holiday and a seaside holiday.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
  • Do you prefer to spend your holidays near the sea or in the mountains?
  • What is the first thing you put in your suitcase when you pack?
🔵 Round 1 — Advantages of Short Holidays▲
Debate
Analyze the advantages of both perspectives.
A: Taking a short holiday allows us to travel more frequently during the year.
B: A long vacation allows us to completely relax and forget about work.
★ How many short holidays did you take last year?
A: A short weekend trip is much easier to plan and prepare for.
B: A long summer trip is more satisfying because we can stay in one place.
★ Do you enjoy planning all details of your travel, or do you decide on the spot?
A: It is much easier to pack a small bag for a short trip.
B: Packing a large suitcase allows you to bring all your favorite clothes.
★ Can you travel for a weekend with just a small backpack?
A: Short trips allow us to explore many different destinations.
B: A long trip lets us learn about local culture and language.
★ What is your favorite nearby city to visit for a quick weekend?
A: We can easily stay active and energetic during a quick vacation.
B: A long vacation gives us enough time to do active sports and lazy resting.
★ Do you prefer walking a lot on holiday, or resting by the pool?
A: Short holidays are usually cheaper and easier to afford.
B: A long trip is a great way to celebrate a special moment in life.
★ Would you choose a cheap weekend getaway or save for a big trip?
A: You do not need to take many days off from work or school.
B: A long trip lets you completely turn off your computer and phone.
★ Is it easy for you to turn off your phone when you are on vacation?
A: It is easy to find a friend to look after your pets for a weekend.
B: You can bring your pets with you on a long road trip.
★ Do you have any pets? Who takes care of them when you travel?
A: Short trips bring quick moments of happiness to break the daily routine.
B: A long trip creates deep, life-long memories with your family.
★ What is your favorite travel memory from your childhood?
A: We can go on a spontaneous trip without heavy stress.
B: We can travel to distant, exotic countries that require long flights.
★ Would you prefer to visit a nearby beach or a distant country?

🟢 Round 2 — Advantages of Long Holidays▲
Debate
Analyze the disadvantages of both perspectives.
A: Short holidays are too brief, and you feel sad when they end so quickly.
B: Long holidays can be very expensive, requiring months of saving money.
★ Have you ever felt that a weekend trip was over before you even started relaxing?
A: Planning many short trips throughout the year can be very stressful.
B: Planning a long itinerary can be complex and lead to arguments with travel partners.
★ Have you ever had a disagreement with someone you were traveling with?
A: Travel fatigue from quick journeys can make you feel more tired.
B: Returning to work after a long vacation is very difficult and causes post-travel blues.
★ Have you ever returned from a holiday feeling like you need another holiday to rest?
A: You cannot fully experience a country's culture in just a few days.
B: Being away from home for a long time can make you feel homesick.
★ Do you get homesick easily when you are away from your home?
A: Frequent short trips can end up being more expensive in total due to multiple flights.
B: If you don't like the destination, a long holiday can feel like a waste of time.
★ Have you ever visited a highly-rated place that you actually did not enjoy?
A: Short trips don't give you enough time to make deep connections with locals.
B: Long trips can cause cultural fatigue from being in an unfamiliar environment.
★ Do you find it difficult to connect with local people during trips?
A: Spontaneous short travel can suffer from bad weather or poor hotel availability.
B: Meticulous long planning leaves very little room for spontaneous changes.
★ How do you handle it when the weather ruins your outdoor travel plans?
A: Quick weekend packing means you often forget essential items.
B: Carrying a massive, heavy suitcase is uncomfortable and exhausting.
★ What is the most important item you have ever forgotten to pack?
A: Short trips can make you feel like you are always on the move without resting.
B: Spending weeks in close quarters can strain your relationship with travel partners.
★ What qualities make someone the perfect travel partner for you?
A: Multiple short flights are highly detrimental to the environment.
B: Being absent from work for weeks can lead to an overwhelming backlog of tasks.
★ How do you prepare your workplace and colleagues before you leave for a break?
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
I am prefer long holidays. → I prefer long holidays. (Verb conjugation check: do not use 'am' before simple active verbs.)
It was a very good trip. → It was a wonderful / fantastic trip. (Style: use more expressive adjectives like 'wonderful' or 'fantastic'.)
I have been in France last year. → I went to France last year. (Past simple is required with specific past time markers like 'last year'.)
I am looking forward to go on holiday. → I am looking forward to going on holiday. ('Look forward to' is followed by a gerund/noun, not an infinitive.)
