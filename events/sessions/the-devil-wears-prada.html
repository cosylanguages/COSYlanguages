This session explores the iconic fashion comedy-drama, The Devil Wears Prada. We will analyze the high-stress office environment of a fictional fashion magazine in New York City. Through interactive discussions, students will master natural business idioms and modern workplace vocabulary.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Gird
to prepare oneself for a struggle or challenge.
We need to gird ourselves for the upcoming busy fashion week.
Groundbreaking
innovative, pioneering, or introducing new ideas.
She did not think floral prints for spring were groundbreaking.
Clacker
a slang term for a woman wearing high heels that click on the floor.
The clackers were rushing around the hallway of the office.
Skimp
to spend less money, time, or care on something than is needed.
Do not skimp on the quality of fabric for the new designs.
Demanding
needing a lot of time, attention, or energy.
Working for a high-profile editor is extremely demanding.
Crucial
extremely important or necessary.
Clear communication is crucial when managing a fast-paced office.
Ambition
a strong desire to achieve success, power, or wealth.
Her ambition drove her to work late hours in Manhattan.
Resign
to voluntarily leave a job or position.
He decided to resign after finding a better workplace balance.
Tremendous
very great in amount, scale, or intensity.
The film was a tremendous success in theaters worldwide.
Relentless
continuing without becoming weaker, less severe, or slower.
The editor was relentless in her pursuit of perfection.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
Briefly introduce your thoughts on high-fashion magazine culture.
  • Have you ever worked for an extremely difficult manager?
  • Would you change your style of dress to fit in at a prestigious workplace?
🔵 Round 1 — General Discussion▲
Questions
In-depth analysis of workplace choices and success.
How do you usually gird yourself for a highly stressful presentation or interview?
★ What is your ritual before a tough task?
Do you agree that a corporate policy must be groundbreaking to survive in modern markets?
★ When did you last see a groundbreaking product?
Why does the presence of high-fashion clacker shoes symbolize authority in corporate settings?
★ Does dress code affect your professional authority?
Why do startup founders often skimp on employee health benefits to secure capital?
★ Have you ever had to skimp on something to save money?
What is the most demanding part of working under a micromanager?
★ Is your current job demanding?
Why is clear communication crucial for preventing employee burnout?
★ What is the most crucial skill in your career?
Can a person have too much ambition, resulting in toxic behaviors?
★ Do you consider yourself highly ambitious?
Under what specific conditions would you immediately resign from your dream job?
★ Have you ever had to resign unexpectedly?
What are the signs of a tremendous work culture in a global enterprise?
★ Have you experienced tremendous success recently?
How do employees stay motivated when dealing with a relentless series of deadlines?
★ How do you deal with relentless workload?

🟢 Round 2 — Future Dilemmas & Hypotheses▲
Predictions
Evaluate the following future-focused scenarios regarding career and vanity.
In the future, the concept of a relentless boss will be outlawed by global mental health statutes.
To achieve tremendous career success, one must sacrifice physical comfort and personal relationships.
Employees who choose to resign rather than adapt to AI helpers will struggle to find future roles.
A startup's ambition should always focus on ecological sustainability over pure financial profit.
It is crucial for young professionals to change companies every two years to maintain high salary growth.
In highly demanding creative fields, standard eight-hour workdays are completely ineffective.
Many companies will skimp on physical office designs because remote work has proven superior.
The term clacker will be forgotten because high-heeled shoes are becoming obsolete in corporate fashion.
A groundbreaking movie will eventually be written entirely by an AI engine rather than a human screenwriter.
We should gird ourselves for a future where traditional high fashion is replaced by completely virtual clothes.
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
She works very hard for achieve success→She works very hard to achieve success(Use 'to' + infinitive to express purpose)
The boss is more demanding than what I thought→The boss is more demanding than I thought(Exclude 'what' in comparative thought comparison clauses)
I will resign if they skimp always on budget→I will resign if they always skimp on budget(Frequency adverbs come before the main verb)
