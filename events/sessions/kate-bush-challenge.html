Focus

Grief & Euphoric Infatuation

This dedicated special challenge session explores the theatrical and poetic artistry of Kate Bush through two contrasting masterpieces: the anti-war folk ballad "Army Dreamers" and the romantic art-pop track "Oh To Be in Love". Students will study British English vocabulary ranging from military mourning and grief to romantic euphoria and surrender. We will analyze the emotional spectrum of Kate Bush's songwriting and storytelling.

Individual Songs: For deep, dedicated song-by-song sessions, visit Army Dreamers and Oh To Be in Love.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Mourning
the expression of deep sorrow for someone who has died.
The entire nation was in mourning after the loss of their leader.
Grief
deep sorrow, especially that caused by someone's death.
She was overwhelmed with grief after her pet passed away.
Consolation
the comfort received by a person after a loss or disappointment.
Knowing she did her best was her only consolation after the match.
Tragic
causing or characterized by extreme distress or sorrow.
The news of the sudden accident was deeply tragic.
Patriotism
devotion to and vigorous support for one's country.
They showed their intense patriotism by flying the national flag.
Infatuation
an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone.
Her sudden crush on the actor was a typical teenage infatuation.
Euphoria
a feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.
The team experienced pure euphoria after winning the championship.
Vulnerable
exposed to the possibility of being attacked or harmed.
Opening up about your deep fears makes you feel highly vulnerable.
Bewitched
enchanted or delighted as if by magic.
He was completely bewitched by her beautiful singing voice.
Surrender
the action of yielding or giving up control to another.
They refused to surrender their rights during the negotiation.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
Initial reactions to the complex, theatrical atmospheres in Kate Bush's performances.
  • What makes Kate Bush's vocal range and storytelling unique?
  • Do you prefer story-driven ballads or pure emotional anthems?
🔵 Round 1 — Lyric Discussion▲
Questions
Dissect the core theme of the song and practice speaking using the highlighted lyrics.
How do different cultures show respect during a period of public mourning?
★ Have you ever worn dark clothes to a memorial?
What is the healthiest way to process deep grief without shutting out friends?
★ Do you prefer solitude when sad?
What words of consolation can you offer to a friend who lost their job?
★ Are you good at comforting people?
Why is the loss of youthful potential in war so deeply tragic?
★ Have you read any tragic history books?
Where is the line between healthy patriotism and dangerous nationalism?
★ What makes you proud of your country?
How can you distinguish between genuine romantic love and temporary infatuation?
★ Have you ever had a sudden infatuation?
What activities besides music can trigger a state of pure euphoria for you?
★ Do you feel euphoria after exercising?
Why do some people find it extremely difficult to be vulnerable with close friends?
★ Are you comfortable sharing your feelings?
Have you ever been completely bewitched by a beautiful city or landscape?
★ What is the most beautiful city you visited?
When is it appropriate to surrender control of a team project to someone else?
★ Do you like delegating tasks?

🟢 Round 2 — Lyric Deep Dive & Debate▲
Debate
Evaluate these deep and controversial statements inspired by Kate Bush's themes.
In the future, public mourning will be conducted entirely in virtual spaces.
Processing grief is a lifelong journey that never truly ends.
The best consolation for a difficult life is the achievement of material wealth.
All tragic events carry a hidden lesson that makes us stronger.
Teaching intense patriotism in schools is outdated and promotes division.
In the future, modern dating apps will eliminate romantic infatuation by matching profiles scientifically.
The feeling of euphoria is temporary and should not be chased as a primary life goal.
Being completely vulnerable is a strategic mistake in professional relationships.
People who are easily bewitched by charisma are highly vulnerable to manipulation.
To find true happiness, we must learn to surrender our personal expectations.
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
She was in mourning of her grandmother → She was in mourning for her grandmother (The correct preposition is 'mourning for' someone.)
She was bewitched from her beautiful voice → She was bewitched by her beautiful voice (Use 'bewitched by' to indicate the source of enchantment.)
They refused to surrender under their opponents → They refused to surrender to their opponents (Use the preposition 'to' after 'surrender'.)
