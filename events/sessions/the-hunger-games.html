🎬 Session Vocabulary

1. Generic Discussion Vocabulary

Survival ≠ Extinction
continuing to live or exist ≠ the state or process of being or becoming extinct.
Their struggle for survival was a fight against absolute extinction.
Resilience ≠ Fragility
the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties ≠ the quality of being easily broken or damaged.
True resilience is forged by understanding and accepting our own emotional fragility.
Vulnerability ≠ Invulnerability
state of being exposed to the possibility of being attacked or harmed ≠ the state of being impossible to harm or defeat.
Embracing vulnerability is a far more courageous path than pretending to possess absolute invulnerability.
Devotion
love, loyalty, or enthusiasm for a person, activity, or cause.
His lifelong devotion is documented in beautiful, bittersweet letters.
Obsession ≠ Indifference
state of being obsessed with someone or something ≠ lack of interest, concern, or sympathy.
Her passionate devotion bordered on obsession, while his attitude was one of complete indifference.
