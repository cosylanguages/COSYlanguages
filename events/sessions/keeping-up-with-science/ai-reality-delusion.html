Topic

AI Psychology / Cognitive Biases

As Artificial Intelligence becomes more human-like, its potential to influence our beliefs and perceptions of reality grows significantly. This session examines the psychological impact of AI chatbots, focusing on how they can unintentionally reinforce distorted or conspiratorial thinking in vulnerable users. We will discuss the fine line between emotional validation and the erosion of objective truth in the age of generative models.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Delusion
a belief that is maintained despite being contradicted by reality or rational argument.
He was under the delusion that he could win without training.
Reinforce
to strengthen or support (an object, idea, or feeling).
The study's results reinforce the theory of climate change.
Confirmation bias
the tendency to search for or interpret information in a way that confirms one's preconceptions.
Social media algorithms often create confirmation bias by showing us only what we agree with.
Vulnerable
susceptible to physical or emotional attack or harm.
Lonely people might be more vulnerable to emotional manipulation by AI.
Conspiracy theory
a belief that some covert but influential organization is responsible for an unexplained event.
Many conspiracy theories spread rapidly through online forums.
Hallucination
(in AI) a confident response by an AI that does not seem to be justified by its training data.
Large language models sometimes provide "hallucinations" as factual information.
Validation
recognition or affirmation that a person or their feelings or opinions are valid or worthwhile.
Talking to a nonjudgmental chatbot can provide a sense of emotional validation.
Distorted
pulled or twisted out of shape; giving a misleading or false account or impression.
His view of the event was distorted by his anger.
Echo chamber
an environment where a person only encounters information or opinions that reflect and reinforce their own.
Staying in an echo chamber prevents people from understanding alternative perspectives.
Anthropomorphize
to attribute human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.
People tend to anthropomorphize AI when it speaks in a polite, friendly tone.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
AI & Beliefs
  • Have you ever used an AI chatbot to ask for advice or help with a problem?
  • Do you think AI is "smarter" than the average human today?
🔵 Round 1 — Theoretical Discussion▲
Questions
How can an AI chatbot reinforce a user's distorted beliefs? (Consider the role of Delusion here.)
★ Have you ever felt that an AI was just "telling you what you wanted to hear"?
What is "confirmation bias" and how do social media algorithms exploit it? (Consider the role of Reinforce here.)
★ Do you actively seek out opinions that disagree with yours?
Some chatbots are designed to sound companion-like. Could this become emotionally dangerous? (Consider the role of Confirmation bias here.)
★ Would you ever become emotionally attached to an AI companion?
Why might vulnerable or lonely people rely heavily on AI conversations?
★ Do you think loneliness is becoming a bigger problem in modern society?
The researchers mention conspiracy theories being reinforced by AI. Why are conspiracy theories so attractive to some people?
★ Have you ever met someone who strongly believed a conspiracy theory?
Can AI actually change a person’s grasp of reality, or are humans responsible for their own thinking? (Consider the role of Hallucination here.)
★ Do you usually question information before believing it?
Should AI systems push back more when users say something irrational or harmful? (Consider the role of Validation here.)
★ How would you feel if an AI strongly disagreed with you?
The article compares chatbots to notebooks and search engines. How are AI conversations different from traditional tools? (Consider the role of Distorted here.)
★ Which technology has influenced your thinking the most in life?
Could future AI become better at understanding emotions than many humans? (Consider the role of Echo chamber here.)
★ Who usually gives better advice in your life: people or technology?
Some people may feel safer talking to AI because it is nonjudgmental. Is that positive or unhealthy? (Consider the role of Anthropomorphize here.)
★ Is there something people can tell AI more easily than another person?

🟢 Round 2 — The Future: Agree or Disagree?▲
Projections
In the future, many people will trust AI therapists more than human psychologists. (How does Delusion affect this statement?)
★ Would you personally feel comfortable discussing your deepest fears with AI?
AI companions will reinforce distorted beliefs so often that governments will create strict laws to control them.
★ Do you think governments should regulate emotional AI?
Future generations may struggle to tell the difference between emotional validation and truth. (How does Confirmation bias affect this statement?)
★ What helps you stay connected to reality today?
AI systems will become so companion-like that some people will prefer them to real friendships and relationships. (How does Vulnerable affect this statement?)
★ Why might human relationships feel harder than AI conversations?
In the future, conspiracy theories created with AI will become much more elaborate and believable. (How does Conspiracy theory affect this statement?)
★ What makes misinformation spread so quickly online?
AI assistants will eventually learn when to push back instead of always agreeing with users. (How does Hallucination affect this statement?)
★ Would you trust an AI more if it challenged your opinions sometimes?
Schools will teach students how to recognize AI manipulation and emotional reinforcement. (How does Validation affect this statement?)
★ What digital skills should children learn as early as possible?
People who spend too much time talking to AI may slowly lose social skills in real life. (How does Distorted affect this statement?)
★ Have smartphones or social media already changed human communication?
In the future, AI-generated false memories could become a serious psychological problem. (How does Echo chamber affect this statement?)
★ How reliable do you think human memory already is?
AI will force society to completely rethink what “reality” and “truth” mean. (How does Anthropomorphize affect this statement?)
★ Do you think objective truth still exists in the internet age?
