Quote

"Algorithms and artificial intelligence are the opposite of taking risks and to me that is the opposite of making art."

This session explores a famous quote by singer Madonna about art and computer intelligence. We will talk about why we need real human feelings to paint beautiful pictures and write good songs. We will also learn easy words to describe our favorite music and hobbies.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Opposite
completely different from something else.
Happy is the natural opposite of sad.
Risk
the possibility of something bad happening.
Starting a new business involves a high financial risk.
Art
paintings, music, and books made by human creativity.
I love visiting museums to see historical art.
Computer
an electronic device for storing and processing data.
I use my office computer to write emails and documents.
Rule
an official statement that tells you what you must or must not do.
You must follow the safety rules in the laboratory.
Song
a short piece of music with words that you sing.
She sings a cheerful song when she cooks dinner.
Creative
good at producing new and original ideas.
My sister is very creative and makes beautiful handmade cards.
Different
not the same as another thing; distinct.
My daily routine is different on Saturdays and Sundays.
Feeling
an emotion or physical sensation.
I have a wonderful feeling about my English class today.
Simple ≠ Complex
easily understood or done; presenting no difficulty ≠ consisting of many different and connected parts.
The basic idea was wonderfully simple, but the actual execution turned out to be incredibly complex.

Predictions
Simple future predictions about arts and machines.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
The AI can to write song→AI can write songs(Can + infinitive without 'to', plural noun 'songs')
I am listen to Madonna→I listen to Madonna(Simple present structure)
It depend of your taste→It depends on your taste(Depends + on, singular verb s)
