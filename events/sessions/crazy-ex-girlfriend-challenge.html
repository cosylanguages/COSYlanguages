📖 Session Vocabulary▲
Kernels
small, soft, or edible parts of a nut, seed, or corn; metaphorically, tiny bits.
He only threw her a few kernels of affection, keeping her wanting more.
Delusional
characterized by or holding idiosyncratic beliefs or impressions that are contradicted by reality.
He was delusional to think he could win without practicing at all.
Obsession ≠ Indifference

Let's Generalize About Men

Resources

Music Video 🎥 Song Link (Backup) 🎵

📖 Session Vocabulary▲
Generalize
make a general or broad statement by inferring from specific cases.
It is unfair to generalize about an entire group of people based on one person's actions.
Satire
the use of humor, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize people's stupidity.
The show is a brilliant satire of modern television culture.
Condescending
having or showing a feeling of patronizing superiority.
He spoke in a condescending tone that offended everyone in the room.

Overzealous

Her compulsive shopping habits led to significant financial difficulties.
