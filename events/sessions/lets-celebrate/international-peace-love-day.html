Full Info 📖

This session celebrates International Peace and Love Day, focusing on global harmony and positive connection. We will discuss the origins of this festive day and how small acts of kindness can foster a sense of community. The conversation will encourage participants to share personal stories of gratitude, compassion, and reconciliation.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Harmony
the state of being in agreement or concord.
Music has the unique power to bring people together in harmony.
Compassion
sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings or misfortunes of others.
Showing compassion to strangers can brighten their entire day.
Reconciliation
the restoration of friendly relations.
The peace talks aimed at the reconciliation of the two countries.
Gratitude
the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation.
Writing down three things you are thankful for is a great exercise in gratitude.
Kindness
the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate.
A small act of kindness can make a big difference in a crisis.
Foster
to encourage or promote the development of something.
We must foster a warm atmosphere in our language classroom.
Gesture
a movement or action performed to express an idea or feeling.
Offering a cup of tea is a simple gesture of welcome.
Global
relating to the whole world; worldwide.
Environmental protection is a global issue that requires cooperation.
Festival
a day or period of celebration.
The city hosts a popular arts festival every summer.
Ritual
a solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order.
Drinking coffee in the morning is a comforting daily ritual.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
Short introduction to gestures of friendship.
  • What is the most positive festival or holiday in your country?
  • Do you think there should be more international days dedicated to peace?
🔵 Round 1 — General Discussion▲
Questions
Explore how celebrations help build connections.
How does promoting harmony benefit local neighborhoods?
★ When do you feel most in harmony with your environment?
Why is showing compassion crucial during difficult social crises?
★ Have you ever received unexpected compassion from a stranger?
What are the key steps in achieving a personal or political reconciliation?
★ Is it easy for you to forgive someone after an argument?
How does practicing gratitude affect our long-term happiness?
★ What is one thing you feel deep gratitude for today?
Why are acts of kindness contagious?
★ What was the last simple act of kindness you performed?
How can communities foster a stronger sense of mutual aid?
★ How connected do you feel to the community where you live?
What simple gesture of friendship is universal across cultures?
★ What gestures of body language make you feel welcome?
How do global events shape our views of other countries?
★ Have you ever participated in a global project or event?
How does celebrating a festival preserve a community's culture?
★ What is your favorite traditional festival to celebrate?
Why do humans need a daily or yearly ritual to feel secure?
★ What is your most important daily ritual?

🟢 Round 2 — The Future: Agree or Disagree?▲
Projections
Discuss these predictions about world peace and global festivals.
In the future, we will have a unified global holiday where all businesses close for a day of peace.
★ If you had 24 hours of absolute global quiet, how would you spend that day?
The internet will eventually foster a single, global culture with shared traditions, making local cultures obsolete.
★ Which of your local traditions would you fight to preserve in a completely globalized world?
As society becomes more digital, traditional community rituals will disappear completely.
★ Have you already replaced any physical, offline rituals with digital or online ones?
Teaching empathy and compassion in schools will become far more important than teaching traditional academic subjects.
★ What is a lesson in empathy you wish you had been taught during your school years?
We will see fewer local conflicts as international communication and instant translation improve.
★ Do you find it easier to resolve conflicts online or face-to-face?
Global environmental crises will eventually force all competitive nations to live in perfect harmony.
★ Does the thought of shared global challenges bring you hope or anxiety?
National borders will become completely open and obsolete by the end of this century.
★ How does your sense of identity change when you travel outside your home country?
Acts of kindness and support done in virtual spaces will become just as powerful as those done in physical person.
★ Has an online interaction ever deeply comforted you during a difficult personal time?
A future world with complete global peace and no competition will eventually become stagnant and uncreative.
★ Do you need a bit of internal friction or conflict to feel motivated to create?
We will eventually develop technologies that allow us to achieve deep personal peace of mind without relying on external relationships.
★ Would you prefer a pill that guarantees inner peace, or do you value the struggle of finding it naturally?
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
We must to keep the peace→We must keep the peace(Must + infinitive without 'to')
I make an act of kindness→I do an act of kindness(Collocation with 'do')
I am full of gratitude for you→I am full of gratitude to you(Preposition 'to' after gratitude)
