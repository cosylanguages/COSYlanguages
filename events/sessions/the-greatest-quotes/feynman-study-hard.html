Quote

"Study hard what interests you the most in the most undisciplined, irrelevant, and original manner possible."

This session explores Richard Feynman's quote about learning with freedom and passion. We will discuss why studying what we love is the best way to learn new things. We will also share our own favorite topics and how we study them at home.

☕ The Philosophers' Ledger: Café Debates

"Let us see how many interpretations exist! There is never only one correct answer to any profound human question." — Oscar Wilde

📖 Session Vocabulary

Interest
to make someone want to know or learn about something.
Science topics always interest me a lot.
Original
new, fresh, and different from other things.
He has a very original style of painting.
Manner
the way in which something is done or happens.
She speaks in a polite and friendly manner.
Learn
to get knowledge or skill in a new subject.
I want to learn how to play the guitar.
Subject
an area of study that you learn at school.
English is my favorite subject at school.
Passion
a very strong feeling of liking or loving something.
Cooking is his absolute passion in life.
Freedom ≠ Slavery
the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants ≠ the state of being a slave or completely controlled.
They fought courageously to defend their freedom and escape from a lifetime of cruel slavery.
Independent
free from outside control; not relying on others.
Learning on your own makes you an independent student.
Creative
having good and original ideas.
She uses creative ways to teach new vocabulary.
Method
a systematic way of doing something.
I tried a new study method to prepare for exams.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
Introduce your learning style.
  • What do you like to study in your free time?
  • Do you prefer to study in a quiet library or at home?
🔵 Round 1 — General Discussion▲
Questions
Simple discussion about studying and passion.
What does Feynman mean when he says we should study what interests us?
★ What is the most interesting thing you have learned this month?
Why is it important to have an original style of learning?
★ Do you have a unique or original hobby?
How does our study manner change when we study alone?
★ What is your preferred manner of studying new languages?
Why do some people find it hard to learn new skills?
★ What is the hardest skill you have ever had to learn?
What is your favorite school subject?
★ Why did you choose this favorite subject when you were younger?
Why is it useful to have a passion in life?
★ Do you share your main passion with your family?
Do students need more freedom at school?
★ If you had total freedom tomorrow, what would you study first?
How can we become more independent learners?
★ Do you study English independently or with a teacher?
How can we be more creative when studying?
★ What is the most creative project you have done?
Which study method is most popular in your country?
★ What method do you use to remember new words?

🟢 Round 2 — Simple Future Discussion▲
Predictions
Simple future predictions about education systems.
In the future, physical schools will not exist; everyone will study at home.
★ How would you personally adapt if Interest became entirely obsolete in tomorrow's world?
Students will choose 100% of their subjects without any mandatory classes.
★ Would you be willing to use technology to artificially boost your capacity for Original?
Learning a language will take only a few hours with direct brain uploads.
★ How do you want future generations of your family to remember your relationship with Manner?
We will not have exams or grades in future university programs.
★ If you could design a futuristic school, how would you teach the value of Learn to children?
All children will learn how to write computer programs before they learn how to read.
★ Do you fear a future where Subject is fully managed or monitored by digital algorithms?
Creative thinking will become more important than memorizing facts.
★ How do you personally prepare for a future where Passion is increasingly rare?
We will study on our own using personal AI tutors.
★ If you could send a single message about Freedom to people living 100 years in the future, what would it be?
Every student will receive a certificate based on their personal passion.
★ Would you choose to live in a futuristic utopia that completely guarantees Independent at the cost of your freedom?
People will learn completely new careers every ten years.
★ What role do you hope Creative will play in your own personal future over the next decade?
We will study science using immersive virtual reality worlds.
★ If a machine could perfectly replicate your experience of Method, would you still value it?
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
I want to study more hard→I want to study harder(Comparative form of 'hard' is 'harder')
I am interest in Feynman→I am interested in Feynman(Use passive participle adjective 'interested')
She learn English fastly→She learns English quickly(Third-person singular 's', adverb 'quickly')
