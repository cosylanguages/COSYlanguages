Quote

“None of us really have a choice in anything but we do have a choice to be ourselves. Stay free”

This session explores the profound idea that true freedom begins with the choice to be ourselves despite external pressures. We will delve into the concepts of authenticity and social conformity, examining why it is often difficult to remain independent in a world of expectations. Participants will discuss the balance between societal roles and personal identity in their quest to "stay free".

☕ The Philosophers' Ledger: Café Debates

"Let us see how many interpretations exist! There is never only one correct answer to any profound human question." — Oscar Wilde

📖 Session Vocabulary

Authentic
being real and true to your own personality, values, and beliefs.
He stayed authentic even when others wanted him to change.
Identity
the qualities, beliefs, and characteristics that make a person who they are.
Traveling helped her understand her identity better.
Pressure
influence or force that encourages someone to behave in a certain way.
Many teenagers feel pressure to fit in with their peers.
Conform
to change your behavior or opinions to match those of a group.
He refused to conform to social expectations.
Independent
able to think, act, and make decisions without relying on others.
She became more independent after moving to another city.
Values
important principles or beliefs that guide a person's decisions and actions.
Honesty and respect are among his strongest values.
Expectation
a belief that something should happen or that someone should behave in a particular way.
There is often an expectation that young people attend university.
Individuality
the qualities and characteristics that make someone unique.
The company encourages employees to express their individuality.
Freedom ≠ Slavery
the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants ≠ the state of being a slave or completely controlled.
They fought courageously to defend their freedom and escape from a lifetime of cruel slavery.
Self-expression
the act of showing your thoughts, feelings, or personality through words, actions, or creativity.
Art gives people a powerful way of self-expression.

🎙️ Discussion Structure

🟠 Warm-up▲
Choice & Authenticity
  • What does "staying free" mean to you personally?
  • Do you think we have as much choice in life as we believe?
🔵 Round 1 — General Discussion▲
Interpretations
Do you think it's possible to "be ourselves" 100% of the time, especially when considering the role of Authentic?
★ When do you feel most like your true self?
How do social expectations conflict with individual freedom, especially when considering the role of Identity?
★ Have you ever made a choice just to please your family or society?
What is the difference between "being free" and "having choices", especially when considering the role of Pressure?
★ Can you feel free even when you have limited options?
How does technology influence our ability to stay authentic, especially when considering the role of Conform?
★ Do you feel a need to perform or filter yourself on social media?
Why do we often feel we "have no choice" in our daily responsibilities, especially when considering the role of Independent?
★ What part of your daily routine feels like a real choice to you?
Can someone be authentic in a career that requires strict conformity, especially when considering the role of Values?
★ Have you ever had to choose between social conformity and personal Values?
How does travel help people discover their true identity, especially when considering the role of Expectation?
★ Who in your life best embodies the essence of Expectation, and what have they taught you?
Is "staying free" a selfish act or a necessary one for mental health, especially when considering the role of Individuality?
★ In what area of your life do you feel Individuality is most desperately needed right now?
What are the long-term consequences of constantly conforming to others, especially when considering the role of Freedom?
★ How has your personal understanding of Freedom shifted over the last five years?
If you could change one life decision to be more "yourself," which would it be, especially when considering the role of Self-expression?
★ If you had to describe your personal experience with Self-expression in one word, what would it be?

🟢 Round 2 — The Future: Agree or Disagree?▲
Debate / Deep Dive
In the future, technology will enable us to digitize and share our experience of Authentic directly. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ How would you personally adapt if Authentic became entirely obsolete in tomorrow's world?
The rapid speed of modern society will eventually make Identity an obsolete concept. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ Would you be willing to use technology to artificially boost your capacity for Identity?
Future educational systems will prioritize the cultivation of Pressure over traditional academic skills. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ How do you want future generations of your family to remember your relationship with Pressure?
As artificial intelligence advances, machines will exhibit a superior capacity for Conform compared to humans. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ If you could design a futuristic school, how would you teach the value of Conform to children?
In the coming decades, Independent will become a highly commercialized luxury commodity. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ Do you fear a future where Independent is fully managed or monitored by digital algorithms?
The survival of human civilization will depend on our collective ability to practice Values. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ How do you personally prepare for a future where Values is increasingly rare?
Future generations will look back at our current understanding of Expectation with deep skepticism. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ If you could send a single message about Expectation to people living 100 years in the future, what would it be?
Global digital connectivity will make true Individuality incredibly difficult to maintain in the future. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ Would you choose to live in a futuristic utopia that completely guarantees Individuality at the cost of your freedom?
In the future, social status will be determined by a person's capacity for Freedom rather than wealth. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ What role do you hope Freedom will play in your own personal future over the next decade?
A world fully governed by logic will have no space left for the mystery of Self-expression. — Do you agree or disagree?
★ If a machine could perfectly replicate your experience of Self-expression, would you still value it?
