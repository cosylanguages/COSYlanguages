Quote

"Knowledge isn't free. You have to pay attention."

This session explores Richard Feynman's quote about the cost of learning. We will talk about how we can focus better when we study. We will also share what we do to remember new words at home.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Knowledge
information, skills, and understanding that you get through learning.
Reading books is an excellent way to get new knowledge.
Attention
listening or looking carefully at someone or something.
Please pay attention to the teacher when she explains.
Free
costing no money; not needing payment.
This English website has a lot of free exercises.
Focus
to give all your attention to one thing.
I need to focus on my vocabulary list.
Remember
to keep in your mind; not forget.
Do you remember the meaning of the word 'focus'?
Learn
to get knowledge or skill in a new subject.
I want to learn how to speak Spanish.
Simple ≠ Complex
easily understood or done; presenting no difficulty ≠ consisting of many different and connected parts.
The basic idea was wonderfully simple, but the actual execution turned out to be incredibly complex.
Mind
the part of a person that thinks, feels, and remembers.
Meditation helps to calm the busy mind.
Share
to tell other people about your ideas or feelings.
I would like to share my study tips with you.
Difficult
hard to do, understand, or deal with; not easy.
This exercise is too difficult for me.

Questions

What is the difference between a free course and a paid course?

How do you stay focused when you study?
★ Where do you go when you need to focus on your homework?
Why is it hard to remember new words?
★ Do you have a special way to remember vocabulary?

★ Why do you want to learn English?
Do you prefer simple exercises or complex ones?

How does learning change our mind?
★ What is on your mind today?
Why is it good to share our ideas with others?
★ Would you like to share your favorite book with the group?
What makes English spelling so difficult?
★ What is the most difficult part of English for you?

🟢 Round 2 — Simple Future Discussion▲
Predictions
Simple future predictions about studying.
In the future, we will learn everything using virtual reality headsets.
★ How would you personally adapt if Knowledge became entirely obsolete in tomorrow's world?
All schools will be free for everyone in the world.
★ Would you be willing to use technology to artificially boost your capacity for Attention?
Computers will do all the difficult tasks at school.
★ How do you want future generations of your family to remember your relationship with Free?
We will remember everything we read with brain chips.
★ If you could design a futuristic school, how would you teach the value of Focus to children?
We will not need to focus because AI will do the work.
★ Do you fear a future where Remember is fully managed or monitored by digital algorithms?
Children will start learning languages at age two using smart games.
★ How do you personally prepare for a future where Learn is increasingly rare?
We will have a single global language in 50 years.
★ If you could send a single message about Simple to people living 100 years in the future, what would it be?
Traditional physical books will disappear completely in 10 years.
★ Would you choose to live in a futuristic utopia that completely guarantees Mind at the cost of your freedom?
People will study science by playing immersive games.
★ What role do you hope Share will play in your own personal future over the next decade?
AI tutors will become our best friends and teachers.
★ If a machine could perfectly replicate your experience of Difficult, would you still value it?
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
I want to study more hard→I want to study harder(Comparative form of 'hard' is 'harder')
I am study every day→I study every day(Simple present structure)
It depend of your focus→It depends on your focus(Depends + on, singular s)
