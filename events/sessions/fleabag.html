This session analyzes the brilliant award-winning British series, Fleabag. We will dissect the subtle art of fourth-wall breaks and sharp-tongued dialogs. Students will explore natural colloquialisms, dark humor, and complex modern emotional vocabulary.

📖 Session Vocabulary

Shambles
a state of total disorder, chaos, or mess.
Her personal life was a complete shambles.
Dry run
a rehearsal or practice session before a real event.
Let us do a quick dry run of the presentation.
Fancy
to be sexually attracted to someone, or to desire something.
She realized she began to fancy her best friend.
Shag
a British slang term meaning to have sex.
They had a casual shag after the party.
Outrageous
shockingly bad, unconventional, or bold.
Her comments during the family dinner were outrageous.
Grieve
to feel intense sorrow or distress, especially after a loss.
It takes a long time to grieve a close friend.
Awkward
causing or feeling embarrassment or inconvenience.
The silence in the café became incredibly awkward.
Dysfunctional
not operating normally or properly; unbalanced.
They come from a highly dysfunctional family structure.
Confess
to admit that one has committed a crime or done something wrong.
He decided to confess his secret to the priest.
Vulnerable
susceptible to physical or emotional attack or harm.
Speaking openly made her feel incredibly vulnerable.

🟢 Round 2 — Advanced Hypotheses & Social Trends▲
Predictions
Critically evaluate these statements regarding modern social patterns.
Being vulnerable online will eventually be replaced by perfectly curated digital personas.
We should always confess our deepest insecurities to prevent mental burnouts.
Most modern relationships are fundamentally dysfunctional due to technology distractions.
Social media makes physical dating encounters feel more awkward than ever before.
Society will always judge individuals who choose to grieve in unconventional or public ways.
An outrageous artistic performance is the only way to spark true social revolution today.
The concept of a casual shag will lose popularity as people seek deeper offline connections.
Most adults secretly fancy a lifestyle that is completely different from their current reality.
Performing a dry run of every life decision ruins the beauty of spontaneous experiences.
A life in total shambles often serves as the perfect catalyst for complete self-recreation.
✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
I fancy to go to a London pub tonight→I fancy going to a London pub tonight(Use gerund '-ing' after the verb 'fancy')
Her life was complete shambles→Her life was a complete shambles('Shambles' is singular and requires the indefinite article 'a')
He was grieved about his friend's loss→He was grieving his friend's loss(Use active continuous form for ongoing emotional process)
