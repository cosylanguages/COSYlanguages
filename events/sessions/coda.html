1. Generic Discussion Vocabulary

Audition
an interview for a particular role as a singer, actor, or musician.
Her emotional college audition is the turning point of the film.
Illness ≠ Wellness
a disease or period of sickness affecting the body or mind ≠ the state of being in good health.
The clinic focuses on healing chronic illness and promoting lifelong wellness.
Apocalypse
the complete final destruction or collapse of the world.
The survivors search for meaning and warmth after the sudden apocalypse.
Immortality ≠ Mortality
the ability to live forever ≠ the state of being subject to death.
The vampire enjoys the gift of immortality, while lamenting the mortality of his human friends.
Infidelity
the action of being unfaithful to a spouse or partner.
The plot unravels through discoveries of mutual marital infidelity.
