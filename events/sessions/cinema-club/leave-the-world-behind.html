✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
I recommend you to see this film→I recommend that you see this filmUse 'recommend that + subjunctive/bare infinitive' or 'recommend doing'.
The reason is because she was afraid→The reason is that she was afraidAvoid 'the reason is because'; use 'the reason is that' for formal/style clarity.
Seldom I have seen such an ending→Seldom have I seen such an endingNegative adverbs like 'seldom' at the beginning of a clause trigger subject-auxiliary inversion.
