🔵 Part 1 — Dialogue & Themes (10 Items)▲
Screenplay Discussion
Deconstruct the character conversations and thematic developments. Actively practice these target terms.
How does the concept of High school stereotypes influence the choices of characters in the opening scenes of Glee?
★ Have you ever experienced a similar situation where High school stereotypes played a significant role?
In what ways does musical performance drive the narrative conflict between the main characters of Glee?
★ How would you react if you encountered the same dilemma regarding musical performance in your own life?
How does the screenplay of Glee use bullying to challenge or subvert the audience's expectations?
★ Do you find bullying to be a relatable theme in modern society, or is it unique to the world of the film?
Which pivotal character in Glee is most defined by Cinematography, and how does this shape their destiny?
★ If you were in the protagonist's shoes, would you let Cinematography dictate your final decision?
How does Narrative pacing symbolize the larger social, cultural, or political context depicted in Glee?
★ What personal values do you associate with Narrative pacing, and how do they compare to the film's perspective?
Can you highlight a specific dialogue in Glee where Show choir becomes the central point of tension?
★ Can you share a time when a conversation about Show choir changed your mind about someone?
What are the immediate or long-term dramatic consequences of slushie in the middle acts of Glee?
★ Have you ever witnessed the unexpected consequences of slushie in your professional or personal life?
How does the visual directing, lighting, or setting of Glee enhance the emotional weight of underdog?
★ Does the emotional atmosphere of underdog in this scene resonate with your own memories or experiences?
In your opinion, does Glee portray diva as a positive force of growth or as a destructive weakness?
★ Do you agree with how the film defines diva, or do you have a different personal interpretation?
How does the resolution of Glee address, resolve, or leave open-ended the conflicts surrounding Opening scene?
★ How has watching Glee changed your outlook on the importance or role of Opening scene?

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
We are living here since two years→We have been living here for two yearsUse present perfect continuous with 'for' to express duration.
They don't allow her leave the house→They don't allow her to leave the houseUse 'allow someone to do something' construction.
He is making her to believe lies→He is making her believe liesUse the bare infinitive after 'make someone do something'.
