❤️ Core Concepts & Feelings

deal
an agreement entered into by two or more parties for their mutual benefit, or a situation.
Finding a nice apartment in this city for a reasonable price is a great deal.
heal
become sound or healthy again.
Proper rest and nutrition will help your body heal much faster.
day
a period of twenty-four hours, or the time of light.
It was a beautiful day to spend outdoors in the countryside.
shame
a painful feeling of humiliation or distress caused by the consciousness of wrong or foolish behavior.
There is absolutely no shame in asking for help when you need it.
better
of a more excellent or effective type or quality.
She was feeling much better after taking a short, peaceful nap.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
