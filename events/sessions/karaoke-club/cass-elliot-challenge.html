💬 Stage 4: Discussion▲

🔵 Round 1 — Lyric Analysis & Discussion

How does 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' by Cass Elliot explore the deeper significance of Individuality in our daily lives?
★ When was the last time you felt a strong sense of Individuality in your own life?
In 'Make Your Own Kind of Music', is Belated presented as a source of strength or a source of doubt?
★ Do you find it easy or difficult to share your feelings of Belated with others?
Analyzing the lyrics of Cass Elliot, how does Melody shape the overall message of the song?
★ How has your personal understanding of Melody changed over the past few years?
In what ways does 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' show that Choir is essential for personal growth?
★ In your daily routine, what helps you maintain your focus on Choir?
How does the musical style of 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' enhance the emotional impact of Chorus?
★ What is the most important lesson you have learned about Chorus?
When Cass Elliot sings about their feelings, does Solo feel like a natural state or a challenge?
★ Do you believe that modern society values Solo, or is it often ignored?
Does 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' suggest that we must work to achieve Inspiration, or does it happen naturally?
★ Who is the person in your life that best represents the idea of Inspiration?
How does the visual imagery of 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' visualize the concept of Peer?
★ If you could gift more Peer to someone you care about, who would it be?
In your opinion, does the protagonist in 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' find peace through Path, or are they left with questions?
★ Do you think that Path is more about personal peace or shared experiences?
How can we apply the lessons of Harmony from 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' to improve our modern lives?
★ What small practice or habit helps you cultivate Harmony in your relationships?

🟢 Round 2 — Broad Themes & Philosophy Debate

The central portrayal of Individuality in 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' is a beautiful reflection of modern human struggles. Let's debate.
Without a deep understanding of Belated, we can never achieve lasting success and happiness. Share your thoughts.
Cass Elliot's work shows that our collective obsession with Melody can sometimes lead to self-doubt. Defend or oppose.
True Choir can only exist when we are completely comfortable with who we are. Discuss.
The music of 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' suggests that we must be willing to take risks to fully experience Chorus. Do you agree?
In the modern world, the quiet development of Solo is often threatened by constant distractions. Discuss.
We cannot truly appreciate Inspiration until we have experienced moments of challenge and failure. Analyze this.
Cass Elliot warns us that focusing too much on Peer can make us lose sight of other practical goals. Share your view.
The ending of 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' proves that accepting our own Path is the key to personal freedom. Discuss.
If we make our life decisions based entirely on the pursuit of Harmony, we risk losing stability. Discuss.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
