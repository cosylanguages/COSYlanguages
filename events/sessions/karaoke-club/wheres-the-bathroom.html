❤️ Core Concepts & Feelings

bathroom
a room containing a toilet and sink, and typically also a bathtub or shower.
He asked for directions to the nearest bathroom in the museum.
dirty
not clean; covered or marked with an unclean substance.
The dirty dishes were piled high in the kitchen sink.
apartment
a suite of rooms forming one residence, typically in a building.
They recently moved into a spacious apartment on the fifth floor.
pictures
paintings, drawings, or photographs on a wall or screen.
The walls of her office were decorated with beautiful pictures of nature.
wash
clean with water and, typically, soap or detergent.
It is important to wash your hands thoroughly before preparing food.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
