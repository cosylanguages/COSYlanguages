❤️ Core Concepts & Feelings

Covina
a city in California, used here as a symbol of suburban life.
He spent his childhood years living in the quiet suburbs of Covina.
Opposite: Metropolis
lonely
sad because one has no friends or company.
She felt a bit lonely living in the giant, unfamiliar city.
lies
false statements made with the intent to deceive.
Basing a friendship on lies will eventually lead to disappointment.
leave
go away from.
They decided to leave the party before it got too late.
find
discover or perceive by chance or after deliberate search.
It takes time to find a career path that truly inspires you.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
