🟢 Round 2 — Broad Themes & Philosophy Debate

Without a willingness to embrace our own flaws, true personal Belated is completely impossible. Share your thoughts.

True Choir can only be achieved when we completely stop caring about the approval of others. Let's debate.
The ending of 'Make Your Own Kind of Music' proves that our personal Chorus is worth losing friends and breaking connections for. Do you agree?
Modern culture focuses too much on instant success rather than the slow, quiet journey of Solo. Discuss.
We cannot find genuine Inspiration without first experiencing moments of deep failure and doubt. Analyze this.
Cass Elliot warns us that focusing too much on our own Peer can make us selfish and blind to others' needs. Share your perspective.
The ultimate test of our Path is how we treat those who cannot offer us anything in return. Discuss.
If we guide our life decisions entirely by our desire for personal Harmony, we risk never finding stability. Discuss.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
