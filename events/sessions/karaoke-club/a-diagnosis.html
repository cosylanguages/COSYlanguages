📚 Stage 2: Vocabulary▲

❤️ Core Concepts & Feelings

diagnosis
the identification of the nature of an illness or other problem by examination of the symptoms.
The doctor provided a clear diagnosis after reviewing the test results.
neurosis
a relatively mild mental illness that is not caused by organic disease, involving symptoms of stress.
He suffered from a mild neurosis that made him overly anxious about cleanliness.
label
a classifying phrase or name applied to a person or thing.
She refused to let any simple label define her complex personality.
free
able to act or be done as one wishes; not under the control of another.
They felt completely free as they hiked through the wide open fields.
supposed
generally assumed or expected to be or do something.
We are supposed to submit our assignments by Friday evening.

✏️ Teacher's Note (Linguistic Corrections)▲
There is a big improvement of my English → There is a big improvement in my English (Use 'in' after 'improvement' to express domain.)
It was a great relief of hearing the news → It was a great relief to hear the news (Use 'relief' + infinitive 'to hear'.)
She is in the choir since three years → She has been in the choir for three years (Use present perfect + 'for' to show duration of state.)
