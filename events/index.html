💡 What we do

Two ways to connect

Whether you want to debate deep topics or enjoy a movie, we have a session for you.

Speaking Club

A small group session (4–6 people) on a real-world topic chosen in advance. The teacher facilitates, corrects lightly, and keeps everyone speaking.

  • Languages English, French, Italian, Russian, Greek
  • Duration 60 min
  • Frequency Weekly
👇 See all 6 clubs
🎬

Cinema Night

Watch a short film or scene in the target language, then discuss it as a group with the teacher. Focus on listening comprehension and natural reaction vocabulary.

  • Languages English, French, Italian, Russian, Spanish
  • Duration 90 min
  • Frequency Monthly
🍿 Enter Cinema Club
🎤

Karaoke Night

Learn languages through the power of music! Sing along to timeless songs, analyze rich slang words, master native accents, and discuss cultural contexts with a native COSYlanguages teacher.

  • Languages English, French, Russian, Greek
  • Duration 60 min
  • Frequency Bi-weekly
