How COSYlanguages works

Three steps from first contact to your first real conversation in a new language.

1

Tell us where you are

Message us on WhatsApp or Telegram. We have a friendly chat about your level, your goals and how much time you have. No tests, no forms.

💡 Tips for language learners

🔑

Consistency beats intensity

5–10 minutes every day is far more effective than 2 hours once a week. Small daily habits compound faster than you think.

🗣️

Always say it out loud

Repeat words and sentences aloud — not just in your head. Speaking trains your mouth and your ear at the same time.

❌

Mistakes are proof you're trying

Every error tells you exactly what to practise next. Our practice mode has unlimited retries — keep going.

🧠

Context beats memorisation

Learn words in sentences, not lists. Your brain remembers meaning. Every COSY lesson introduces words through real communication.
