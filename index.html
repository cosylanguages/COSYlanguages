Learner's Tips 💡

Consistency is Key 🔑

5-10 minutes every day is much more effective than 2 hours once a week. Keep that streak alive!

Say it Out Loud 🗣️

Always repeat words and sentences out loud. It helps your brain and muscles get used to new sounds.

Don't Fear Mistakes ❌

Mistakes are proof that you are trying! Our practice mode allows unlimited retries, so keep going.
